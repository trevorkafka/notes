# What is *Trevor's Notes*?

My name is **Trevor Kafka**, I am a math and physics tutor, and <span style="font-family: Freeman; font-size: 110%; font-style: italic">Trevor's Notes</span> is a project that began in **July 2025**. If you are interested in learning more about the tutoring classes that I offer or are interested in signing up for a tutoring lesson, please visit my main website at www.trevorkafka.com.

<span style="font-family: Freeman; font-size: 110%; font-style: italic">Trevor's Notes</span> serves as a way for me to post detailed information in the form of brief help articles about topics that I teach during tutoring sessions to help anyone on the internet (including, but not limited to, past, current, and future students of mine).
