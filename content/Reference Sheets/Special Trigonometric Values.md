Below are the key degree-radian conversions and special sine, cosine, and tangent values that all trigonometry students should know. 
$$
\begin{array}{cc|ccc}
\text{degrees} & \text{radians} & \text{sin} & \text{cos} & \text{tan} \\ \hline
0^\circ & \vphantom{\frac{\sqrt 3}{2}} 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\[0.5em]
30^\circ & \frac{\pi}{6} & \frac{1}{2} & \frac{\sqrt 3}{2} & \frac{\sqrt 3}{3} \\[0.5em]
45^\circ & \frac{\pi}{4} & \frac{\sqrt 2}{2} & \frac{\sqrt 2}{2} & 1 \\[0.5em]
60^\circ & \frac{\pi}{3} & \frac{\sqrt 3}{2} & \frac{1}{2} & \sqrt{3} \\[0.5em]
90^\circ & \frac{\pi}{2} & \vphantom{\frac{\sqrt 3}{2}} 1 & 0 & \text{undef.}
\end{array}
$$
